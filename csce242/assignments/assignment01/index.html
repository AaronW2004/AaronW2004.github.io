Animatronics: Bringing Characters to Life

Animatronics refers to the use of robotic devices to emulate a human or an animal, or bring lifelike characteristics to an otherwise inanimate object. These are widely used in movies, theme parks, and various entertainment venues to create realistic movements and interactions. The technology behind animatronics has evolved over the years, incorporating advances in robotics, mechanics, and materials science.

From simple mechanical puppets to complex robots with artificial intelligence, animatronics has captivated audiences for decades. The development of these technologies requires collaboration across multiple fields, including engineering, art, and storytelling. Today, animatronics continues to push the boundaries of what is possible, creating lifelike characters that can interact with people in real-time.
