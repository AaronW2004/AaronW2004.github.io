Baccalaureate

Saturday, May 11 · 11:00 AM
The Baccalaureate Service is a spiritually enriching ceremony that invites students, faculty, families, and friends to reflect on the journey to graduation. Rooted in Spelman’s core values, this interfaith event fosters unity, purpose, and gratitude through music, spoken word, and messages of encouragement from esteemed guests.
Saturday, May 11 · 11:00 AM · Chapel Hall
View Commencement Schedule
Baccalaureate Service held in Georgia International Convention Center in College Park, Georgia.
This year's speaker is Rev. Dr. Angela D. Sims, a nationally recognized theologian and Spelman alumna. Her message will focus on legacy, leadership, and the transformative power of faith in action.
